Installation Instructions

Equipment

  1. Energy Lens Android App
  2. ACME sensor for each item or set of items you wish to track
  3. QR Codes to tape to each item

Setup

  1. Print your QR codes from the energy lens site. It is suggested that you print the QR codes on stickers.
  2. Attach a QR code for each item you want to audit.
  3. The basic gestures fall into three categories: registration, linking, and acquisition. Registration. It consist of a single tag swipe and entering information through the screen. Linking gestures consist of two swipes and the press of button to conÔ¨Årm the un/linking.
  4. Open the Energy Lens Mobile Application and register the QR code by scanning the QR code with your phones camera.
